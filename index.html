About

Rooted in Tradition, Grown for the World

Born in the fertile soils of Degloor, Vyankyaa Foods has spent over a decade bridging India's finest farmers with the global marketplace. Founded by Baalaje V. Kadaam, we are more than exporters — we are storytellers of the soil.

Every shipment carries the care of a farmer's hands, a season's patience, and the promise of quality that has made us trusted across 50+ nations.
