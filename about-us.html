Our Beginning

From Jalna to the World

It began with a single product: Kardi oil. My father didn’t have a global vision back then; he had a work ethic that belonged to the soil. He believed that if it came from our farm, it had to be the best. That humble origin in the markets of Jalna is the DNA of every shipment we send today.

Transitioning into the second generation, Omkar Gadghile didn’t just inherit a business; he inherited a responsibility. He saw that the world was hungry for the integrity of Indian farming. He expanded our footprint from oils to grains, seeds, and spices, scaling that same 'farm-to-hand' honesty into a global operation that now serves over 50 nations.

We don't just move goods; we move the story of Indian agriculture forward.

The Founder

Omkar Gadghile

Omkar Gadghile is the second-generation architect of Bhavya’s global expansion. Taking the torch from a legacy of local trade, he has aggressively pioneered a multi-sector approach, integrating tech-driven logistics with traditional Indian farming wisdom. Under his vision, Bhavya Agro has evolved from a regional oil supplier into a global export authority, trusted by importers across five continents for consistency and uncompromising quality. He works directly with 200+ farm partners, ensuring that the ambition of the company never outpaces the welfare of the farmer.

"Exporting India’s Best Flavors to the World — that's not just our tagline, it's our daily commitment."
