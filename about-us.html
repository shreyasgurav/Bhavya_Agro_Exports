Our Beginning

Honest Roots, Global Reach

Our story began in the 1990s with the raw hustle of a single product: Safflower oil. Our first-generation founder started with nothing but a commitment to honest trade and the soil of Jalna. He believed that trade wasn't just about moving goods, but about upholding the integrity of the farmer—a value that remains the bedrock of Bhavya Agro Exports.

For over 30 years, that initial spark evolved organically. As the business transitioned to the second generation, Omkar Gadghile didn't just inherit a company; he inherited a legacy of trust. He saw that the world was ready for Indian agriculture at scale and aggressively expanded our footprint into multiple sectors, from premium oils to global grain exports.

Today, Omkar leads with the same founding DNA but a different, global-first ambition. He has transformed a regional trading house into a multi-sector export authority, scaling the same 'handshake-integrity' of the 90s into a worldwide operation serving over 50 nations.
