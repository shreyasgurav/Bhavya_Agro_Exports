Our QA Process

How We Maintain Quality

01

Farm-Level Inspection

Our agronomists visit partner farms during the growing season to monitor cultivation practices, pesticide usage, and early harvest quality indicators.

02

Grading & Sorting

Post-harvest produce undergoes stringent grading for size, colour, brix, and defect tolerance — only Grade A produce is cleared for export.

03

Lab Testing

Batches are tested at NABL-accredited laboratories for pesticide residue, microbial load, and product-specific quality parameters before packing.

04

Pre-Shipment Inspection

A final inspection is conducted at the packhouse before loading. Documentation is cross-checked with actual cargo for 100% compliance.

05

Cold Chain Management

Temperature-controlled packaging, pre-cooling, and reefer containers ensure product integrity from packhouse to destination port.

06

Post-Arrival Support

We stay in contact with buyers post-arrival. Any quality feedback is incorporated into our next-cycle protocols for continuous improvement.
